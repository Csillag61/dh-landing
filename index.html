Frequently Asked

Learn from the questions of our showcased companies, setting you up for excellence.

1. How many videos do we give you?

We will repurpose however many existing videos you have to be used on our platform. From your videos, we create 12-16 videos to show job seekers. Learn more.

2. How many candidates do I receive?

Companies receive at least 3 vetted candidates per week. If you want more, simply mark it in your settings! Learn more.

3. What are the videos used for?

The collection of videos are used to fill out your Company Profile, and shown to Job Seekers. Job Seekers that are most interested in your videos are then asked your Top 3 screening questions. We work with companies who wish to control what content is shown on the platform. Learn more.

4. How quickly do I receive my candidates?

We have hundreds of job seekers using our platform daily. We give you the best match as quickly as possible - most companies receive vetted candidates within 2 days! Learn more.
