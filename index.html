Frequently Asked

Learn from the questions of our showcased companies, setting you up for excellence.

1. How many videos do you make?

We create at least 12 videos about your company and open job positions. These videos are all about getting an 'inside look' at your workplace. Learn more.

2. How many candidates do I receive?

Companies receive at least 3 vetted candidates per week. If you want more, simply mark it in your settings! Learn more.

3. What are the videos used for?

We create videos about each job position you are hiring for, as well as a profile on your company. These collection of videos are used to fill out your Company Profile, and shown to Job Seekers. Job Seekers that are most interested in your videos are then asked your Top 3 screening questions. Learn more.

4. How quickly do I receive my candidates?

We have hundreds of job seekers using our platform daily. We give you the best match as quickly as possible - most companies receive candidates within a few days! Learn more.
